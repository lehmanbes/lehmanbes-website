About BES

Who We Are

In the 21st century, addressing the world’s most pervasive environmental problems will increasingly rely upon comprehensive and integrated strategies developed by experts across academic disciplines in collaboration with public, private, and government stakeholders. Funded by the Alfred P. Sloan Foundation, the Bronx Environmental Summit serves as a platform for members of the Lehman College and Bronx communities to engage with environmental experts from academia and the public, private, and nonprofit sectors for a timely conversation about climate change, environmental equity, and the emergent skills required for students who are interested in pursuing environmental careers.

Inspiring keynote speakers and panelists with a variety of disciplinary backgrounds, professional experiences, and technical expertise will discuss the pressing cross-scale, crosscutting, and compound environmental and societal effects of global climate change and innovative solutions to these challenges.
